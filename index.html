Biography

Ngoc-Quan Ha-Phan is currently pursuing his Master’s degree in the ANDA Lab, Soongsil University, Seoul, South Korea, under the supervision of Prof. Myungsik Yoo. He received his Bachelor of Engineering degree in Automotive Engineering from the Ho Chi Minh City University of Technology and Education, Vietnam, in 2023. His research interests focus on robotics, autonomous vehicles, and vehicle perception systems, with particular attention to driving scene depiction, environment perception, and object-level reasoning for intelligent transportation systems.
