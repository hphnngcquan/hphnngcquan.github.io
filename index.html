About Me

Ngoc-Quan Ha-Phan is a master’s student supervised by Prof. Myungsik Yoo at the ANDA Lab, Soongsil University, Seoul, South Korea. He earned his B.Eng. degree in Automotive Engineering from the Ho Chi Minh City University of Technology and Education, Vietnam, in 2023. His research interests include robotics, autonomous vehicles, and vehicle perception engineering.
